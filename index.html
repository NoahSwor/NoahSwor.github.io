Cubing

in my free time, I practice solving the Rubik's Cube
Competitions are sanctioned by the World Cube Association with hundreds of thousands of participants worldwide.
You can learn more about cubing through the documentary The Speed Cubers (2020) on Netflix.
My proudest accomplishments across my 7 years of cubing are:

  • A 2nd place in the world blindfolded solve of 15 seconds in 2022.
  • A silver medal in fewest moves at the North American Championships in 2024.
  • Bronze medals in blindfolded at the 2022 and 2024 North American Championships.
  • The current 8th best all rounder in the world, being well ranked in all 17 officially recognized events.
outside of competing, I enjoy making youtube videos talking about how to practice, and interviewing other top competitors.
